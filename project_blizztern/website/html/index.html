This website has three main goals:

2.Internship promotion: I wanted to extend the blizzard's internship program form a bit, thus giving me another chance to make a good (or maybe bad) impression on the people reviewing my application.

Q2: Who are you?

Depending who you ask you may get a wildly different answer. To some I'm a genius, to others - a complete idiot. Truth is, I'm somewhere in the middle and I'm constantly shifting between either.

My name is Vasil Kolev, I'm 20 years old and I'm currently in university. Formerly I was a student at the Technical school "Electrical Systems" or "ELSYS" where I got most of my skills from. Now I'm looking towards my distant future and planning my steps to success. I've always dreamt of working at Blizzard and it's "about damn time" I started to work towards that.

I have some useful skills under my belt.

As a programmer I have worked with C, C++, Java, MathLab, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, Ruby and a little bit of Assembly. Most of these I've used during High School. As a hobbyist I can make some really simple electrical circuits and can program a microcontroller to do something.

Q7: Why work at Blizzard?

I love everything that you guys do! Since I was little I loved watching my brother play Starcraft: Brood War and though he totally nuked me every single time I played against him I still loved every minute of it. Then Warcraft 3 and WoW WotLK were the go-to games of my teen years. Then recently I've been playing HS, HotS and Legion. I love Overwatch though I'm still playing only on the free weekends. (I was going to get it this Black Friday but I got carried away working on this website and forgot...).

Every single game is a classic, every single one is a guide for "How to make great games" and I want to be a part of that. Working at Blizzard is a dream of mine since I first asked myself the question "what do you want to do for a living?" at 7th grade. Since then the answer is still the same - "I want to help Blizzard make great stuff".
